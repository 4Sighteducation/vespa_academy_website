VISION

odyssey

1  ​a long wandering or voyage usually marked by many changes of fortune

 

2 an intellectual or spiritual wandering or quest

 

We can all choose what that journey is...

...but as we get older, we're sometimes more reluctant to make those choices

Need convincing?  This book is designed for toddlers.  They LOVE making choices AND they're brave enough to make unconventional ones...

Recapture that bravery!  What would the 5 year old you choose?

Bill Burnett, a professor of engineering, and Dave Evans, cofounder of games company, Electronic Arts, run a Stanford University course called 'Designing Your Life'.  It helps students develop their vision of what they want to achieve... and get there.  

Imagine three possible future lives, each one with a different timeline.  Imagine yourself five years into the future on each of those different paths your life might take.

The three possible 5 year paths

The current path

This is what you think you're most likely to do.  It's the path you're currently on.  You'll finish your courses, get a job, find a training course or go to university.

1.

The plan B

This is what you'd do if timeline 1 wasn't possible.  You don't get that uni offer or you don't get the job you apply for.  Reimagine your career plan.  Make it completely different to timeline 1.

2.

The wild card

Forget money, expectations, parents' hopes, ability.  You have total freedom to do what YOU want.  What would the next five years look like?
