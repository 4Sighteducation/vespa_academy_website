A DOZEN NOTICEBOARDS

In schools and colleges up and down the country, in corridors, receptions, community spaces
and classrooms, there are noticeboards.

Some of them display student work, some have sports results or calendars… but many of them have opportunities.

In this experiment, you’re going to wander around the campus and look at twelve different
noticeboards...

..really take notice of the things on there – read the flyers, look at the posters, make a note of the clubs, competitions, societies or classes that are advertised there.

Once you've tried the noticeboards, start looking for opportunities in other places....

eg. Online competitions, Academic, Sports or Music Scholarships, Holiday Workshops, Volunteering opportunities,  Local clubs, etc, etc......
