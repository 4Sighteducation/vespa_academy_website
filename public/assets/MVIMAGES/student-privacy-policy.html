Your Privacy Matters to Us!

This explains how we keep your information safe when you use VESPA Academy

Quick Summary: We collect some information about you to help improve your learning experience. We keep it safe, we don't sell it to anyone, and your school owns all your data. You can ask to see or delete it anytime!
